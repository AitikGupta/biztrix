Entry-Level Inbound Customer Service Representative (Montreal, Canada)

Job Title: Entry-Level Inbound Customer Service Representative

Location: Montreal, Canada

Our client helps you find and order food no matter your location. Based on your address they will tell you which restaurants deliver to your area as well as a list of restaurants available for pick-up nearby. You also have the option to search specifically by cuisine, restaurant name or menu item. When you find what you're looking for, you can place your order online or by phone, free of charge. Our client also gives you access to reviews, coupons, special deals and a 24/7 customer care team that tracks each order and makes sure you get exactly what you ordered.

 

The Entry-Level Inbound Customer Service Representative's day to day:

  • » Answering the above customer care calls or chats for any customers who may be looking to place an order, track an order, adjust an order, confirm their order or simply answer an inquiry.
  • » Provide real-time updates to restaurant account settings and document any actions taken.
  • » Work with the leadership team to achieve specific call center goals in our pursuit to provide world class customer care.
  • » 30% phone work
  • » 70% chat work

 

The Entry-Level Inbound Customer Service Representative Requirements:

  • » Dynamic and able to work in a team setting as well as independently.
  • » Good English (verbally and written).
  • » Strong typing and grammar skills.
  • » Knowledge of basic computer tools.
  • » Ability to multi-task and manage up to three chats at one time.
  • » Available to work evenings (3:00PM-11:00PM) and weekends.
  • » Punctual with a sense of responsibility.

 

Training:

  • » The training date & time has yet to be confirmed however it will be the last week of February or first week of March.
  • » It is a 5 day training from 1:00pm-9:00pm or 3:00pm-11:00pm (time will be determined closer to training date).

 

The Entry-Level Inbound Customer Service Representative position offers:

  • » Easy work with a great team!
  • » Low stress environment
  • » Permanent positions (minimum of 20 hours per week)
  • » Part timers need to be available either Saturday or Sunday.
  • » Possibilities for growth within our team.
  • » Flexible hours – perfect for students
  • » Paid training
  • » Friendly staff
  • » Continuous training and coaching.
  • » A warm and supporting environment

 

If a dynamic and encouraging environment is what you are looking for, please send us your resume and please mention Job Offer: “ Entry-Level Inbound Customer Service Representative ” along with a short cover letter indicating why you would be considered a good candidate

Telephone Fundraising Representative (Montreal, Canada)

Job Title: Telephone Fundraising Representative

Location: Montreal, Canada

Telephone Fundraising Representative to call supporters on behalf of charities and non-profit organizations.

Our client, Fundraising Direct, provides a multitude of fundraising services for non-profit organizations such as UNICEF and Medecins sans Frontieres. We are currently hiring dynamic and goal oriented telephone fundraising representatives who want a fulfilling and challenging role. As a telephone fundraiser, you will be speaking directly to existing and new donors and, therefore, have the ability to effectively and professionally represent the charities' ideals and goals.

 

Job Requirements:

  • » The telephone fundraising representative will work with a multitude of non-profit organizations;
  • » Has outbound call center experience, ideally in telemarketing or sales;
  • » Is fluent in English, bilingualism would be considered an asset, and will result in $1 more per hour;
  • » Has great communication and rapport skills;
  • » Is available to work during our mentioned hours of operation (see below).

 

Telephone Fundraiser full time requirements:

  • » Available to work a minimum of 37.5hrs/week
  • » Available Monday through Friday 13:30-21:00 and/or Tuesday through Friday 13:30-21:00 & Saturday 10:00-17:00.

 

Telephone Fundraiser part time requirements:

  • » Available to work a minimum of 21hrs/week
  • » Available to work evenings 17:30-21:00
  • » Available to work Saturdays 10:00-17:00

 

Telephone Fundraising hours of operation:

  • » Monday through Friday 13:30-21:00 and Saturdays 10:00-17:30.

 

Telephone fundraiser Training period:

  • » Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday from 11:00-5:00 and Thursday 1:30-9:00.
  • » Availability for this training period is mandatory in order to be employed by Fusion.

 

Telephone Fundraiser position offers:

  • » Dental and Medical benefits after 3 month probation;
  • » Free Wi-Fi during your breaks;
  • » Permanent part-time & full-time positions;
  • » Possibility for growth within our team - all trainers and supervisors started off on the phone, we promote within the company.
  • » A chance to work with well-known organizations dedicated to a great cause;
  • » A beautiful workplace conveniently located in the heart of Old Montreal, a short walk from Place d'Armes metro (on the orange line).

 

If a dynamic and encouraging environment is what you are looking for, please send us your resume specifying the job offer " Telephone Fundraising Representative " along with a short cover letter indicating why you would be considered a good candidate for this telephone fundraising representative position.
